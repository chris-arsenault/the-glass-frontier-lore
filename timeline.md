# Timeline

Major events only. For full context on any event, follow the links. Dates are CE (Common Era, Earth-origin calendar carried forward by the colony).

| Date | Event | Notes |
|------|-------|-------|
| ~2167 CE | **Colony founding** | Terraforming collective establishes Kaleidos. Construction of the orbital ring begins. |
| ~2190s CE | **Ring completion** | Orbital ring fully operational. Integrated crystal lattice handles power, climate, communication, and automation for the entire colony. Ring-era AI custodians are active. |
| ~2230s CE | **Peak ring era** | Colony at its height. Multiple settlements across Kaleidos surface, fully connected through ring infrastructure. Other worlds in the system being settled. |
| **2240 CE** | **[The Glassfall](history/events/the-glassfall.md)** | The orbital ring shatters. Cause unknown — suspected sabotage. Ring fragments drift apart; many survive as isolated habs. Surface infrastructure collapses. Communication severed system-wide. |
| 2240–2305 CE | **The Signal Famine** | ~65 years of isolation. Surface settlements, ring habs, and outer-system worlds lose contact. Cultures diverge. Independent traditions of [resonance](cosmology/resonance.md) use develop. Isolated habs evolve unique cultures and governance systems. |
| ~2260s CE | **Echo Rivers discovered** | Settlers on Kaleidos's surface discover that underground waterways carry [audible fragments](cosmology/echo-rivers.md) of pre-Glassfall transmissions. |
| ~2300s CE | **Reconnection begins** | Long-range communication gradually re-established between surface settlements. Ring habs begin making contact. Trade routes reopen. |
| ~2320s CE | **Verge salvage industry emerges** | Organized operations begin harvesting [ringglass](concepts/ringglass.md) from the debris field. The [future:Verge Compact] forms to regulate. |
| ~2350s CE | **Tempered Accord negotiations** | Multi-year negotiations between surface settlements, ring habs, and outer-system representatives. Attempt to build a shared governance framework for a civilization that spent 60 years becoming strangers. |
| **2369 CE** | **Present day** | The Tempered Accord is the current framework for inter-settlement cooperation. Tensions between expansion and preservation. The [Glass Frontier](cosmology/the-glass-frontier.md) is accessible, inhabited, and slowly falling apart. |
