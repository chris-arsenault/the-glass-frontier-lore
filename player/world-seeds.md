# World Seeds

Ideas, examples, and texture mentioned in conversation that haven't been formalized into entries yet. Not stubs — just seeds for future use. Grouped loosely by category.

## Named Relics

- A **tome that grants good luck** to its readers. How, and for how long, and at what cost — undefined.
- A **spear forged in impossible dimensions**, said to pierce any material. Very hard to store safely because it doesn't fully respect three-dimensional containment.

## Substances / Resources

- **Psychedelic resonance-enhanced honey** — produced by insects nesting near duskgrain deposits. Trace Adversary corruption. Mild euphoria and heightened resonance sensitivity at low doses. Reality perception disorders at sustained use. Black market commodity.
- **Crystallized aura** ("god juice") — some kind of condensed resonance essence. Mentioned but not defined. Possibly related to duskgrain or a distinct phenomenon.
- **Echo River water with original voices intact** — unpurified stillwater sold to collectors. Pre-Glassfall conversations you can listen to. Extreme black market prices.

## Thematic Seeds

- A **hab whose charismatic leader left** — the community was held together by force of personality, and now that person is gone. What does the hab become? Not a tragedy — an evolution. The theme of "what do you build when the original blueprints are gone?" at settlement scale. The opposition expression: maybe the hab is *better* without the leader, in ways nobody expected.
- **The Tuners as reinvention, not recovery** — they didn't get elven science back, they built something different. Folk Tuning vs Clarisant academic Tuning is inheritance vs reinvention playing out in real time. The folk approach is sometimes better for local conditions precisely because it wasn't built from the original theory.

## Locations / Scenarios

- A **secret gambling den** where an artifact lets you gamble with lifespan. Not metaphorical — you literally wager years.
- A **deep-space station held together by Tuner concentration** — if the Tuners stop focusing, the station fails structurally. (Referenced in Tuners entry.)
- Habs where **only Tuners are permitted to live** — entire social structure organized around resonance sensitivity. (Referenced in Tuners entry.)
- Habs that **prosecute unauthorized resonance work** — hidden Tuners as an oppressed class.
- A hab governed by **musical consensus** (Fermata Station — already an entry, but the concept could exist elsewhere in different forms).
- A hab that **let its environmental controls drift** until the interior rewilded into something prehistoric. Jurassic Park style. (Referenced in Glass Frontier entry.)
- The **eighth dimension modifying your hallway** resulting in a flooded living room. There's an insurance form for this.

## Black Market / Criminal

- [future:Coremark] as a criminal corporation — artifact smuggling, duskgrain trafficking.
- Individual Conclave archivists doing **off-the-books relic authentication** for buyers of questionable provenance.
- **Illegal drugs** beyond the honey — unspecified but present.

## Cultural Texture

- **"Elven engineering"** as common shorthand for technology that works better than anyone can explain.
- Building codes that account for **dimensional drift**.
- Insurance that covers **resonance spikes and interdimensional intrusions**.
- Tourist shops in Glasswake selling **overpriced ringglass trinkets stamped "First Signal."**
- **Glasslight** — the Sable Crescent phenomenon where crystal strata refract sunset through swamp mist.

## Faction Texture

- The Conclave's **three failed attempts** to be forced open by the Accord — complied with letter, ignored spirit.
- Lattice Proxy Synod's **two sealed corridors** where expeditions didn't return.
- Synod **rituals that stop working unpredictably** — engineering by superstition.
- Lira Vashtenri's departure from the Conclave as a **personal betrayal dressed as public service**.

## Narrative Structures

- **"The unexpected happens frequently"** — the world's structures exist to handle the improbable, not prevent it.
- **Prominence is not inherited** — a marginal place can host a renowned event (Fermata Open analogy: Shibuya Crossing for anime fans).
- **The honey hook** — player-accessible Adversary corruption that's pleasant until it isn't.
- **Dragon-slaying stories** — cthonic beasts in the Shear exist to support this archetype.
